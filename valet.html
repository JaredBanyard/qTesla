Driving Instructions




Driving:
Step on the brake pedal to turn car on. There is no ignition, start button, or manual parking brake.
The right column stalk controls the direction. Push up for reverse, push down for drive (while stepping on brake pedal).

Parking:
On the right column stalk, press on the tip to engage parking brake.

Locking:
(Put the car in PARK first) Walk away with the key and the car will turn off & may lock.
You can force the car to lock by clicking the top/middle of the key fob once.
There is no off button.
Return to the car with the key and the car will unlock.
If the door handle does not extend, simply touch it.
You can unlock the car by double clicking the top/middle of the key fob.
